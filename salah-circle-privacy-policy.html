Salah Circle

Privacy Policy

Salah Circle helps you track prayer (salah) times, stay connected with family, friends, masjid, and colleague circles, and engage with Islamic content. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Salah Circle, you agree to the collection and use of information as described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1.Information We Collect

Account & Authentication Data. To create and secure your account, we collect:

  • Phone number — used to send a one-time password (OTP) for sign-in. The OTP code itself is not stored after verification.
  • Google account information (if you sign in with Google) — your name, email address, and profile picture, as provided by Google Sign-In.

Location Data. With your permission, we access your device's GPS location to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Location is used in real time to determine prayer timings and is not used for any other purpose.

Profile Information. Your display name and, optionally, a profile photo you upload are stored to personalize your account and circles.

Circle Data. When you create or join a circle (Family, Friends, Masjid, or Colleagues), we store the circle name, type, member list, and your prayer activity as shared within that circle, so members can see each other's prayer status as intended by the feature.

Prayer Tracking Data. We store your daily prayer completion status (e.g., prayed, missed, in progress) so you can track your own prayer history and, where you choose to share it, so circle members can see it.

Device & Notification Data. We collect a device push-notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging) to deliver prayer time reminders and circle activity alerts.

Islamic Content. Content such as Quranic verses, duas, and reminders is delivered to all users and does not require any personal data.

2.How We Use Your Information

  • To create, verify, and secure your account (phone OTP and Google Sign-In)
  • To calculate and display accurate prayer times based on your location
  • To enable circle features — creating, joining, and viewing shared prayer activity
  • To send push notifications for prayer reminders and circle updates
  • To maintain and improve the App's reliability and features
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your data for third-party advertising.

3.How Information Is Shared

Within your circles. Your name, profile photo, and prayer activity are visible to the other members of any circle you join. This visibility is the core purpose of the circle feature and is expected by participants.

4.Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit. We take reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5.Data Retention

We retain your account and circle data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile, prayer history, and circle membership data are removed within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal or security purposes.

6.Your Choices & Rights

  • Location permission can be disabled at any time in your device settings; prayer times will then be unavailable or require manual location entry.
  • Notification permission can be turned off in your device settings to stop prayer reminders.
  • Leaving a circle stops your prayer activity from being visible to that circle's members going forward.
  • Account deletion can be requested at any time by contacting us at the email below; we will delete your associated data as described in Section 5.
  • Access or correction of your profile data can be done directly within the App's profile settings.

7.Children's Privacy

Salah Circle is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8.International Users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, as determined by our service providers (Google Firebase). By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

9.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
